My Services

Handyman for Home Technology and IT

I provide expert IT support, smart home installations, and networking solutions to keep your technology running smoothly at home. Whether you need help setting up smart devices, improving Wi-Fi coverage, or troubleshooting tech issues - I have you covered.

  • Laptop / PC Support

    Do you have a problem on your Windows or Mac computer? Is the computer running slower than usual, are some thing broken? I will fix it for you.

  • Smart Home Technology

    Installation of smart lighting, interior and exterior. Retrofit of zoned central heating followed by automation to suit your lifestyle.

  • WiFi / Cable Networks

    Installation and setup of WiFi Networks. Troubleshooting bad signal. Have you switched supplier, do you need help changing the modem?

  • Smart TV

    Have you just bought a Smart TV? I will set it up, load up Netflix and help you get started.
    I DO NOT WORK WITH "DODGY BOXES"

  • Remote Support

    Similar to Laptop / PC Support, but performed remotely. Can be cost effective for light tasks that can be solved quickly.

  • 24/7 Support

    If you find yourself in an emergency situation and it can't wait until the the next business day, fear not. Contact Me, I am here to help.

  • Home Office Setup

    I will set up home office features, such as secure VPN, cabled network connections. Data backup strategies. Cable organisation. Desk and chair guidance.

  • Computer Sales

    A lot of our customers don't need new computers. I can supply pre-owned refurbished hardware at a fraction of the new price. Perfect for college.

  • DESIGN / BUILD / REPAIR

    Need a new rig for design, gaming or something simple but unique? With many years of experience - I am happy to help you design, build and repair the perfect computer for you.
